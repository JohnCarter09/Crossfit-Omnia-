About Us

We are amazed at how far our gym and community has come in such a short ammount of time. From our roots starting at crossfit Deco here in Denver, to competing at our second crossfit games last summer, we really have had an amazing journey in such a short ammount of time. We pride ourselves on being the best crossfit gym in Denver, CO.

Since 2013 we have been striving to help people live healthier lives inside and outside of the gym. Whoever comes through the doors at Crossfit Omnia is greeted and made to feel right at home. From Our expert coaching to our inclusive facility we continue to set the bar high for all local crossfit affiliates.

At Crossfit Omnia, not only do we cater to the everyday athlete who wants to come in and get a normal workout in each day, we're also home to the 2017 13th fittest team in the world. After a cinderella run at the 2016 CrossFit Games finishing 26th place out of the top 40 teams in the world, CrossFit Omnia's team successfully made it back to the games in 2017.

This year the team headed to Madison, WI to take on the best in the world. After 6 days of competition CrossFit Omnia finished overall amongst the world's best teams and athletes.

Highlighting the trip was a top 3 finish in an event, and FIVE MORE top 10 event finishes.

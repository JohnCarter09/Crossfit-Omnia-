We Offer More Class Options For Our Members

At Crossfit Omnia we offer special classes for all of our athletes at NO additional fee.

Skills Class

Tuesdays from 6-7pm PM

This class was designed to help our members achieve their gymnastics goals. Taught by one of our best coaches, this class will help you improve mobility and get that first muscle-up.

Yoga Class

Tues / Thurs from 8-9pm & 8-9am on Sundays.

Taught by one of Denvers leading yoga instructurs, Tim Murray. This class was designed to help with mobility and give you a break from all those PR's.

Olympic Weightlifting

Thursdays - 5:30-7pm

Crossfit Omnia: A Little History
est. 2013

Sometimes I’m amazed that our gym has come so far in such a short ammount of time. From our roots starting at crossfit Deco here in Denver, to competing at our second crossfit games last summer, we really have had an amazing journey.

Since 2013 we have been striving to help people live healthier lives inside and outside of the gym. Whoever comes through the doors at Crossfit Omnia is made to feel right at home.

Crossfit Omnia does not only cater to the athlete who wants to get a solid workin in each day, we're also home to the 2017 13th fittest team in the world. After a cinderella run at the 2016 CrossFit Games, CrossFit Omnia's team successfully made it back to the games in 2017.

After 6 days of competition, CrossFit Omnia finished 13th amongst the world's best teams and athletes. Congrats to Kelly Stone, Ashley Gaillot, Sarah Manuel, John Carter, John Birkmeier, and Jeff Schuette on their performance at both the 2017 South Regional and the 2017 CrossFit Games.

Come be a part of our team, come be a part of our story.
